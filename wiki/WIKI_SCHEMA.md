# WIKI_SCHEMA

This wiki is a headless Obsidian vault inspired by the Kepano/Karpathy frameworks.

Principles:
- shallow structure: keep folders intentionally simple and avoid deep nesting
- link-heavy: connect notes with wiki links instead of duplicating information
- lowercase-kebab-case filenames: use names like project-status, person-eva, source-book
- aliases: use frontmatter aliases for alternate names, acronyms, and translations
- frontmatter: prefer metadata such as type, title, status, created, updated, tags, aliases
- MOCs (Maps of Content): maintain curated hub notes that index important themes, projects, people, and sources

Suggested folder roles:
- inbox: raw captures and quick notes
- projects: active work with clear outcomes
- people: profiles and relationship notes
- insights: distilled ideas, principles, and reusable thinking
- sources: references, excerpts, links, and evidence
- mocs: maps of content and navigational hubs
- archive: completed or dormant material
- assets: images, files, and attachments
- templates: reusable note structures

This vault is designed to behave like a headless Obsidian vault: the structure and links matter more than any single app UI.
