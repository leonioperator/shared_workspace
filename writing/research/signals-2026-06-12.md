## KKV relevance

- For Hungarian SMBs, the main angle is not "new model hype". The useful message is: better AI now means more reliable delegated work, but price, latency, and vendor rules must be managed.
- The strongest practical opportunity is an "AI operations budget" habit: choose fast/cheap models for routine work, reserve expensive reasoning models for high-value tasks.
- Real-time translation is a near-term business feature for SMEs working with foreign suppliers, customers, or seasonal workers.
- Local transcription is a concrete, low-friction workflow for owners: meeting notes, voice memos, site visit summaries, and customer calls without uploading sensitive content to random tools.
